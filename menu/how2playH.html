How to Play



Game Objective

A forest is on fire! Save the forest before all healthy trees burn up by picking up saplings and plating new trees to replace burning trees



Controls

  • W - Move Up 1 Grid
  • A - Move Left 1 Grid
  • S - Move Down 1 Grid
  • D - Move Down 1 Grid
  • Q - Pick Up Sapling
  • E - Place Sapling
